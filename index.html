Navigator

VHT Navigator™ is a scalable, customer outcome management software solution that captures and analyzes customer-specific activity, navigating effortless customer interaction and desired outcomes for customers and organizations. Brands using Navigator address disconnected, cross-channel customer experiences by connecting key moments and identifying real-time opportunities to guide customers as they move across channels over time in search of the outcomes they need.
